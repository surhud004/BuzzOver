Instructions:

Use a mouse to play the game.

After clicking on Start button:

Place your mouse pointer at beginning of the pipe and hit Enter key.

Re-arrange the pointer position towards left side and hit Enter, if the popup doesn't go away.

Move the mouse pointer through the pipe without touching the pipe walls to win!!
